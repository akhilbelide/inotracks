MS Dhoni, superstar cricketer and captain of the IPL team, Chennai Super Kings (CSK) is riding the popularity wave with his team just having entered the IPL 2018 finals after the nail-biting defeat of Sunrisers Hyderabad. Shane Watson, the Chennai Super Kings (CSK) all-rounder, in a recent press conference before their match against Sunrisers Hyderabad, had high praise for Captain Cool, MS Dhoni. “Just his feel and intuition for the game, I’ve experienced that with a few incredible players I’ve played with, MS is certainly right up there,” said Watson. And while the cricket star has been travelling extensively across India and internationally for T20 and IPL 2018 matches, it’s not a secret that MS Dhoni is immensely fond of his birthplace Ranchi in Jharkhand. That’s perhaps why the cricketer has built his dream home — his biggest house yet — in Ranchi.
